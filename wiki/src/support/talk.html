Support list

[[!img lib/list.png link=no]]

Subscribe to our [[user support mailing list|tails-support]].

This is a public mailing list, so be careful with what you are sending. Give only the necessary informations about yourself, and if possible use Tails or [[Tor Browser|https://torproject.org/torbrowser/]] to hide your IP address.

Chat

[[!img lib/chat.png link=no]]

  • server: irc.oftc.net
  • port: 6697
  • chatroom: #tails
  • use TLS/SSL to connect!
